About me

Curiosity became engineering.

My interest in electronics, robotics, and programming began early and gradually became both my field of study and the way I explore ideas. I like understanding a system across layers: from circuits, sensors, and communication buses to firmware, Linux, C++, FPGA logic, and simulation.

I’m particularly interested in microcontroller applications, automotive and autonomous systems, and space technology. Outside engineering I enjoy drawing, guitar, LEGO, Minecraft, game and mod experiments, and building things simply to discover how they work.
